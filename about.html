About Amrudhya Harvests

Connecting India's purity with global markets through trusted, quality-driven trade

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between India's rich agricultural heritage and global demand for quality products

Our Inspiration

Amrudhya Harvests was founded with a vision to connect the purity of Indian agricultural products with the world. We aim to connect India's pure, natural agri-products with both domestic and global buyers through trusted, quality-driven trade.

Our Vision 2030

To become a trusted brand for rice, pulses, herbs, and spices in 30+ countries across India and internationally, delivering consistently high-quality products with ethical sourcing and dependable service.

What Sets Us Apart

We are a problem-solving company offering pure, lab-tested products with flexible supply and custom solutions — without owning manufacturing, yet maintaining full quality control through our verified partner network.

Our Business Approach

We are a problem-solving company with a product-driven approach. Our goal is to simplify sourcing for our customers — ensuring quality, branding, and delivery without the hassle.

We don't just supply products; we deliver solutions for buyers seeking reliability, flexibility, and trust. Our lean trading model allows us to focus on what matters most: connecting quality Indian agricultural products with satisfied customers worldwide.
